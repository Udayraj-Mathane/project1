Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy, about 8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
  2. Wipe out skillet and place over medium heat.
  3. Place 2 slices of cheese on 1 slice of bread. Top with bacon and tomato slices. Top with remaining 2 slices of cheese and bread.
  4. Spread butter on top and bottom of sandwich.
  5. Grill sandwich in skillet until bread is toasted and cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
